Those are my go-to videos, but I've also tried the Coptic Stitch.

Supplies

To get started you will need the following supplies:

  • Paper (I often use computer paper folded in half)
  • Thread
  • Book Board
  • Bookcloth
  • PVA Glue
  • X-Acto Knife
  • Push Pin
  • Ruler
  • Marker

Never heard of Book Board, Bookcloth, or PVA Glue?! Check out Hollanders! They used to be located in the Kerrytown Market, but are now an online only store.
